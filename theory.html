"Theory Of Uncertainity in Relation"

By Sir Jay Patel

In 2024, An Indian scientist named Sir Jay Patel stated that "It is impossible to determine simultaniously the exact Relation and exact rate in increase of ego (or anger) in the relation between Saumya(Babu) and Vishwa"

Later on some uncertainity was caused in this relation due to some external factors like Mahir Limbachiya, Kairav Panchal, Vedant Bhrambhat and many more.

After seeing all this Bullshit, Sir Jay Patel discovered an eqution to find the exact relation and the rate of increase in the ego of both Saumya and Vishwa.

Sir Jay Patel proposed uncertainity equation as a result of dual behaviour of Saumya and Vishwa.

He named this equation "The Equation of Uncertainity in Relation"

  • Mathematically it can be given by :

Δr ⋅ Δe ≥ i/4π

Where, Δe = nΔx

Δr ⋅ nΔx ≥ i/4π

where,
Δr = change in Relation
Δx = external factors (or EX)
Δe = change in Ego (or anger)
